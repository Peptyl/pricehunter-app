## 🎯 FREEMIUM STRUCTURE

### FREE TIER (Lead Generation)
**Goal:** Show value, convert to Premium

**What's Free:**
- Browse top 3 deals daily
- View price comparisons
- See savings calculations
- Basic search (10 perfumes)
- Manual price check (5/day)

**What's Locked:**
- ❌ Price alerts
- ❌ Deal predictions
- ❌ 6-hour delay on data
- ❌ Limited perfume database

**Conversion Hook:**
> "12 deals found yesterday. You saw 3. Premium users saved £540."

---

### PREMIUM - £4.99/month (Main Revenue)
**Target:** Regular buyers (1-2 bottles/month)

**Features:**
- ✅ Unlimited price alerts
- ✅ **INSTANT alerts** (no delay)
- ✅ All 100+ perfumes
- ✅ AI deal predictions
- ✅ Price history charts
- ✅ Restock alerts
- ✅ Price drop probability
- ✅ Bulk tracking (50 items)

**Value Prop:**
> "One alert pays for 6 months."

**Example:**
- User pays £4.99
- Gets alert: BR540 £50 off
- Saves £50 on one purchase
- ROI: 900%

---

### PRO - £12.99/month (Power Users)
**Target:** Resellers, collectors, influencers

**Features:**
- Everything in Premium
- **VIP early alerts** (1 hour before Premium)
- **Arbitrage finder** (buy low, sell high)
- **WhatsApp/SMS alerts**
- API access
- Portfolio tracking

**Value Prop:**
> "One flip pays for a year."

**Example:**
- Buy BR540 £170 (Poland)
- Sell £245 (UK)
- Profit: £75
- Pays for 5.7 months of Pro

### Stage 1: Discovery (Free)
1. Sees ad: "Creed Aventus £90 off"
2. Clicks, lands on deal page
3. Sees locked deal: "🔒 Sign up to unlock"
4. Enters email (free account)
5. Sees 3 deals, 9 more locked

**Conversion hook:**
> "You missed 12 deals this week. 
> Premium users saved £540."

---

### Stage 2: Engagement (Free)
1. Browses deals daily
2. Searches for specific perfume
3. Hits limit: "5 searches used"
4. Sees upsell: "Unlimited searches with Premium"

**Friction point:** Limits create desire

### Stage 3: Conversion (Premium)

3. Clicks upgrade

5. Gets instant alert, buys fragrance
6. Saves £45 on £4.99 subscription

**Retention hook:** Instant value delivery

---

### Stage 4: Retention (Premium)
1. Daily deal emails

3. "You saved £340 this year" notifications
4. Referral program: "Give £5, Get £5"

**Annual hook:** "Your subscription paid for itself 10x"
