# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 23, 2026  
**Last Updated:** February 23, 2026

PriceHunter ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our mobile application and services.

## 1. What Data We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address
- Password (encrypted)
- Optional profile information

### Usage Data
- Search history and queries
- Products you track or add to your wishlist
- Price alert preferences and settings
- App interaction data (features used, time spent)

### Device Information
- Device type and operating system
- App version
- Unique device identifiers (for push notifications)
- IP address

### Purchase Data
- Products you've marked as purchased
- Retailer links you've clicked (for affiliate tracking)

## 2. How We Use Your Data

### Personalization
- Customize your home feed based on tracked products
- Recommend similar fragrances and deals
- Show relevant price drop alerts

### Service Delivery
- Send price drop notifications via push or email
- Maintain your wishlist and tracked products
- Sync your data across devices

### Analytics & Improvement
- Analyze app usage patterns to improve features
- Monitor app performance and stability
- Understand user preferences for product development

### Communication
- Service-related announcements
- Marketing emails (only with your consent, opt-out available)
- Price alerts you've subscribed to

- **Purpose:** App performance and user experience optimization
- **Data:** Anonymous usage statistics

## 4. Data Storage & Security

- All data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
- We use industry-standard security practices
- Passwords are hashed using bcrypt
- Regular security audits and penetration testing

## 5. Your Rights

### GDPR (European Users)
- **Right to Access:** Request a copy of your data
- **Right to Rectification:** Correct inaccurate information
- **Right to Erasure:** Delete your account and data ("Right to be Forgotten")
- **Right to Restrict Processing:** Limit how we use your data
- **Right to Data Portability:** Export your data in a usable format
- **Right to Object:** Opt-out of marketing and certain processing

### CCPA (California Residents)
- **Right to Know:** See what personal information we collect
- **Right to Delete:** Request deletion of your personal information
- **Right to Opt-Out:** Opt-out of sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
- **Right to Non-Discrimination:** Equal service regardless of privacy choices

### Exercising Your Rights
To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@pricehunter.app. We will respond within 30 days.

## 6. Data Retention

- Account data: Retained until you delete your account
- Search history: Retained for 12 months, then anonymized
- Inactive accounts: May be deleted after 24 months of inactivity (with notice)
- Analytics data: Retained in anonymized form indefinitely

## 7. Children's Privacy

PriceHunter is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If you believe we have collected data from a child, contact us immediately.

## 8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United Kingdom and other countries. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notice. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
