- CTA: "Get Deal Alerts"
- Social proof: "Join 2,000+ fragrance lovers saving £50+ per bottle"

### Section 2: The Problem
- "You walk into Harrods..."
- "See your grail fragrance: £265"
- "Pay it because what choice do you have?"
- "But what if I told you..."

### Section 3: The Solution
- Same fragrance, £175 elsewhere
- Show comparison table
- Explain grey market (not fake, just imported)

### Section 4: How It Works
- 3-step process

- Time to alert: 12pm & 6pm daily

- User testimonials with photos
- "Saved £127 this month" - James

- "It's free. Seriously."

- "Stop overpaying today"
